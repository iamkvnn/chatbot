**What is the OptiSigns OptiStick Digital Signage Player?**

OptiSigns OptiStick Digital Signage Player, aka OptiStick, is the perfect solution for your digital signage needs. It is optimized for digital signage and is fully compatible with all OptiSigns features and apps. The player is easy to set up and can be managed from anywhere

Here are the key reasons you should to consider using an OptiStick over just downloading digital signage apps on the Smart TVs

- With an OptiStick, you will have Auto launch functionality when you start the TV.
- OptiStick will prevent Screen Saver / System Power of TVs to interrupt with signage display
- Large Local Data storage of 16GB compared to few MB on the Smart TVs for your content.

**Key features of OptiSigns OptiStick Digital Signage Player**

- The device is neatly designed and has a small form factor. It can be easily installed on the back of TVs.
- The OptiStick device comes preconfigured, It just takes 1 min to setup, just plug in to the power source. There is no need to create/login to Amazon/Google accounts etc.
- It supports 4K Video
- 16GB storage - enough for large number of images, videos in your playlist
- It has an Ethernet port so you can connect to the wired internet for a more stable internet.
- It supports Mass pre-configuration for large deployments
- It has capability for remotely viewing what’s playing on the device.
- Ability to remotely troubleshooting the devices
