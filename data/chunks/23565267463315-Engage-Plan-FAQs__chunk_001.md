**What is OptiSigns Engage Plan?**

Engage is a new suite of apps and features designed to help you turn screens into two-way communications. It enables the creation of engaging, impressive, and interactive connections with your customers.

Here are a few highlighted features in Engage:

- [Kiosk Designer Pro](/hc/en-us/articles/15313086319763)
- [Content Library Kiosk](/hc/en-us/articles/17604055961747)
- [Lift & Learn](/hc/en-us/articles/13097501958291) (and other IoT Sensor experience)
- [QR Scan-to-Interact:](/hc/en-us/articles/8899727608083) Turn static screens into an interactive experience, no additional sensor, HW needed
- [AI Camera:](/hc/en-us/articles/360058259834) Customize experience based on your viewer, collect data to understand your audience
- [Enterprise App](/hc/en-us/articles/13320135306515)
- [Check-in & SMS follow up](/hc/en-us/articles/23566927217939)

**Can I try it out?**

Yes, you try Engage out at no cost for one screen.

Just log in or sign up for a trial if you don't have an account yet, and go to the Engage page in your dashboard.

![](/hc/article_attachments/23565291422483)

This screen is the only one that will ever have Engage enabled, and it is a one-time assignment. If you remove the Engage feature from that screen, you will not be able to add it to another.

**How much is it?**

Engage plan is $30/mo/screen. With an annual plan, that cost is reduced to $27/mo/screen.
