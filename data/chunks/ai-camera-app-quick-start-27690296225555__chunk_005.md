**4**.) **Set up camera & app on your device**

If you are using Linux or Raspberry Pi, the AI Camera will be automatically install when you click AI Camera Activate in the step above. You may need to restart your device. If you need instruction on how to manually activate, run AI Camera for Linux, you can follow [this guide](/hc/en-us/articles/360060676993-How-to-manually-install-activate-OptiSigns-AI-Add-on-on-Linux-or-Raspberry-Pi).

For Android, you'll need to go to the app permission settings and grant camera access.
