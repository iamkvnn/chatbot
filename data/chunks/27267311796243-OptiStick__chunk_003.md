From here, there are two paths, depending if you pre-configured your device at checkout or not:

- If you **DID** pre-configure your device at checkout:
  - The device should automatically connect to your WiFi. It will take a couple of minutes to set up and start playing content. [Sign-in here](https://app.optisigns.com/signIn) to get started.
- If you **DID NOT** pre-configure your device at checkout:
  - Keep following the guide below.

3. If you see the below screen below please connect to the Network

![WIN_20250331_10_01_57_Pro.jpg](/hc/article_attachments/39894411653395)

4. To configure your Wi-Fi now, press the Ok button on the remote. Network and Internet page will open up. In the Network & Internet, select your Wi-Fi network, and then enter your Wi-Fi password.

![WIN_20250331_10_04_15_Pro.jpg](/hc/article_attachments/39894458704019)

**You should now be connected to the Internet.**

5. You can also connect to Wi-Fi using the following

- Use our Mobile Admin app ([link](https://www.optisigns.com/mobile-app))
- Mass Config with USB, SD Card ([link](/hc/en-us/articles/4416542923667-How-to-perform-mass-provisioning-with-OptiSigns))
- You can also use Ethernet Port on the device and connect to the internet via wired connection.
- If you order an un-provisioned device (the device will not be automatically provisioned to your account because you order as guest, or you just chose to order an un-provisioned device).
