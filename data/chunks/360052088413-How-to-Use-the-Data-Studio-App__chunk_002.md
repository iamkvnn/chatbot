- **Update Interval -** How often OptiSigns will ping your Embed URL to update the data displayed on it. This is measured in seconds.

## Deploying a Data Studio App

You can deploy your new Data Studio app as an individual asset, or as part of a [Split Screen](/hc/en-us/articles/360026559573-How-to-Create-and-Use-the-Split-Screen-App).

To get your new Data Studio asset to a screen, go to the **Screens** tab, then click the screen you want to assign it to.

![](/hc/article_attachments/52550786489747)

This brings up the **Edit Screen** tab:

![](/hc/article_attachments/52550786490387)

Here, select **Asset** under Content type. If you already have an Asset, Playlist, or Schedule selected, you can hit **Change**.

Then, select your created Data Studio Asset:

![](/hc/article_attachments/52550819884563)
